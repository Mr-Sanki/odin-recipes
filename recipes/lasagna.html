Classic Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a hearty and comforting dish that everyone will love!

Ingredients
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (6.5 ounces each) canned tomato sauce
  • 2 cans (14 ounces each) canned tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 3 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Steps
  1. In a large skillet, cook and stir the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain the excess fat.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, canned tomato sauce, sugar, basil, salt, and black pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer with 1/3 of the lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta or cottage cheese, and 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with the remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the lasagna is hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes. Let it stand for 10 minutes before serving.
