Homemade Tomato Soup Recipe

Description

This homemade tomato soup is rich, flavorful, and perfect for warming up on a chilly day!

Ingredients
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) whole peeled tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
Steps
  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic; saut√© until softened.
  2. Add whole peeled tomatoes, breaking them up with a spoon. Stir in vegetable broth, sugar, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 20 minutes.
  3. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup to a blender in batches and blend until smooth.
  4. If desired, stir in heavy cream for a creamy texture. Heat the soup until warmed through.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.
